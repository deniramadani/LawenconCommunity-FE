What are community events?
Our community events are an opportunity to listen to teachers and experts from the British Council answering questions from learners like you. The events take place on Facebook and on YouTube and each one lasts about 30 minutes.
What happens in a live event?
Our team of educators lead a discussion about the topic and then answer questions from here on the course or questions from the viewing audience.
How do I join in?
You can find a list of events on this page. Look at the event details and if the time is suitable for you, just follow the event meeting link
